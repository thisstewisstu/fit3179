Heritage Sites of Government Schools in New South Wales

Symbol Map of New South Wales

This map visualizes the location of heritage sites across New South Wales government schools. Each circle represents a school site, categorized by colour for heritage type and size for their endorsed significance. A large majority of heritage sites are clustered along the coastline, aligning with the fact that many school zones are also located along the coast.

Number of Each Heritage Type Categorised by Endorsed Significance

This stacked bar chart shows the endorsed significance of heritage sites, classified by heritage type. The chart allows users to filter by the type of heritage for a more focused analysis by using a drop-down menu. There is an overwhelming number of heritage sites which are only of endorsed significance of 1, after each site has been carefully assessed. This indicates that most sites are recognised as local heritage.
